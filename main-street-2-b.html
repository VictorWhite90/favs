Property Description

Experience modern luxury in this mint 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom condo, located at the vibrant intersection of Main Street and Front Street.

Bathed in natural light, the apartment features stunning South-facing industrial grid windows, creating a bright and welcoming atmosphere. he primary suite offers ample space, including a walk-in closet, a dedicated dressing and makeup area, and an en-suite marble bathroom with an oversized Jacuzzi tub. Additional features include California Closets, Restoration Hardware light fixtures, Nest thermostats for the central air system, a vented LG washer/dryer, and whitewashed hardwood floors.

The Bridgefront Condominiums, a boutique luxury building in the heart of Dumbo, provides convenience and comfort. Amenities include part-time doorman service (Monday to Friday, 9 am to 5 pm), private basement storage, bike and stroller storage, a health club, and a communal rooftop terrace. A parking garage is conveniently located across the street.
